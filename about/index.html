About Me


I'm Ryan, a technologist whose curiosity was sparked at age 8 by the communications tower in my home suburb of Bald Hills, Queensland.

By 1995, my passion had evolved into creation. I was building websites using HTML and scripting mIRC bots on AustNet, laying the groundwork for a career fusing technology with meaningful innovation.

Decades later, I've progressed from youthful coding to redefining technology as a professional. My work now focuses on developing mmWave 5G radar systems integrated with AI. These systems aren't simply technical achievements; they're tools for understanding the "silent language" of the human body to improve care.
