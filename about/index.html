About Me


I'm Ryan, a technologist whose curiosity was sparked at the age of eight by the communications tower in my home suburb of Bald Hills, Queensland.

By 1995, my passion had evolved into creation. I was building websites using HTML 2.0 and scripting mIRC bots on AusNet—a foundation that led to a career at the intersection of technology and impactful innovation.

Decades later, my experiences have expanded from coding to significant professional achievements in technology. Currently, I work on advanced radar systems that integrate with machine learning and neural networks, redefining how we interact with and benefit from artificial intelligence.

On my blog, I delve into myriad subjects—from the intricate nuances of 5G technology and AI to the very personal stories behind our advancements in aged care. I aspire to demystify the complexities of my work, making it both accessible and enlightening for my readers.
